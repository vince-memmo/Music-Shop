    Music Shop allows you to loop through chords and a drum beat so you can workshop melodies with the keyboard all on your computer - no instruments required
  • When entering a chord, 'Duration' refers to the number of quarter notes that the chord will be played for
  • Each column on the drums represents one quarter note
  • You can add multiple measures at a time by pressing the 'Add Measure' button
  • 'Clear Drums' will empty the queued up drum pattern
  • To play your chord loop, simply press 'Play' above the loop
  • You can mute the whole track, as well as the chords and drums, individually
  • While your loop is playing, use the keyboard to improvise and hone in melodies
  • To play the piano, use the corresponding keyboard keys
  • Music Shop allows up to 12 measures
